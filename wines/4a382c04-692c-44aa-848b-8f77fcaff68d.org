** Ratings

*** 2023-05-06 - ☆ 7.50

Hehe, the first bottle we opened was corked. Luckily there was a second bottle to 'cure this taint'. Not complex and linear yet delicious and mineral Chardonnay. Salted nuts, white chocolate candy filled with marzipan, lemon, and seawater. Flavourful, good structure and acidity.
