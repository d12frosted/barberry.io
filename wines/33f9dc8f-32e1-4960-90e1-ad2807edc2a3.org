** Ratings

*** 2023-06-20 - ☆ 7.50

White pepper, raspberry and wet stones. Not overly complex, but quaffable and delicate. Exactly the kind of rosé wines I love - insanely light red ones. Medium acidity, low tannin, medium-low body. Mineral. Neat. A calmer version of [[barberry:/wines/4caa5436-0c62-488b-902d-a4669eb97dc1][Névrosé]].
