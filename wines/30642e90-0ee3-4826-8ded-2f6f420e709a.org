** Ratings

*** 2022-11-20 - ☆ 7.50

Cake 'golden key' mixed with citron (so think about crème brûlée, citrus, cream and bakery). It even shows some oxidative notes (like baked apples) and aromas of blue cheese. Good volume, acidity and texture. Slightly too sweet for my taste, but it's round, easy and friendly. Nice.
