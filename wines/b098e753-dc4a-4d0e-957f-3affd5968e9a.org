** Ratings

*** 2023-02-14 - ☆ 7.50

Unexpectedly good. I am going to take a few bottles and serve one of them blindly to my friends. So be prepared. Unlike most other wines by Beykush, this one is not over-oaked. Charming nose with ripe wild cherry, fig, red flowers, and coffee. Quite juicy, medium to full body with good tannin. The aftertaste is rather long and fruitful (man, the black currant juice). Definitely worth checking.
