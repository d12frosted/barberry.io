** Ratings

*** 2022-09-02 - ☆ 7.25

Nero di Troia? On a hot evening? Ah, by Valentina Passalacqua? Of course, give me that damn bottle! Not complex, yet delicate and delicious.  Strawberries stew, earth, and some yellow fruits accompanied by Sicilian orange. Need to taste it again.

*** 2022-11-02 - ☆ 7.25

Maybe it's not the best weather to drink this wine, but someone entered the room with a catchy phrase about scum bombing our infrastructure, and this bottle was ready to save the mood. Sicilian orange, forest berries, good structure and quaffable profile. What can I say? Not complex, but honestly good.
