** Ratings

*** 2022-11-25 - ☆ 7.50

Good Médoc. Slightly tired but pleasant to drink, especially combined with tartar. Casis marmalade delight covered in sugar, mildew, wet earth-covered mushrooms and an old dried humidor. Round, well balanced, though it lacks acidity. Very friendly wine.
