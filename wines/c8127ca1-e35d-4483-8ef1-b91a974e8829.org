** Ratings

*** 2023-04-06 - ☆ 8.00

Wonderful Chenin Blanc. Fruit-forward bouquet of quince, white flowers (like acacia and tilia blossoms) and sea pebbles. Sophisticated yet too young both in the nose and mouthfeel. Less convincing and nuanced than, say, Les Hauts de Husseau. Yet charming, balanced and mineral.
