100% Chardonnay; south-west-facing single-vineyard Dizy of 1 hectare on the boundary with Aÿ. The vines are planted in 1960 on millstone-grit gravel over clayey marl, followed by Campanian chalk. Vinification and ageing in oak casks on the less. Zero dosage. 3368 regular bottles and 200 magnums produced.
