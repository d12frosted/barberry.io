** Ratings

*** 2023-01-24 - ☆ 7.40

Once I got home, I decided to revive myself with a bottle of a still Xarel-lo. Simple and slightly nervously fizzy. Lemon, sea breeze and stones. A lovely combination that I usually enjoy so much. But here, it just lacked confidence. Especially on the palate. As straight as an arrow, it penetrates my senses and leaves a fresh aftertaste with little to no flavour. It's not bad, but it didn't deliver. Or maybe it was a mistake to open it after such a long day.
