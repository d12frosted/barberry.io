** Ratings

*** 2020-11-24 - ☆ 7.00

Simple but so enjoyable and gulpable wine. It comes in 1 litre format for a reason, you know. Compote made from mix of red and black berries. Juicy, refreshing with long aftertaste. Give me an informal occasion, and I will brighten it with this wine.

*** 2020-12-26 - ☆ 7.00

In my sense, perfect table wine that you can enjoy from any glassware with or without food. It is not pretentious and honestly delivers. Black and red berries compote, species. Refreshing, juicy.
