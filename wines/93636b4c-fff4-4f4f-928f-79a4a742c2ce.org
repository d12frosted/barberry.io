** Ratings

*** 2021-11-29 - ☆ 9.50

I consider myself lucky to taste 20 years old Gran Reserva Blanco. Fantastic experience. Interestingly, it is not as pompous as recent Reserva release. While being rich in the nose, it is restrained and more delicate. Complex bouquet: apple candy, dried grass, fennel, mushrooms, Jerez, dried dough, honey and white flowers. Multilayered and fresh palate, with nice salty lemon touch. Great and demanding wine. And it still has potential.
