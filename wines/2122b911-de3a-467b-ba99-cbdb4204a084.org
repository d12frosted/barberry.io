** Ratings

*** 2022-09-15 - ☆ 7.75

One more beauty to act as a gateway to the world of 'natural wines'. It's bright, it's juicy, it's fruity, and it offers some crazy notes. Dark cherry, bruised cherry, dark berries, and hair spray. VA is not disturbing here; it only increases complexity. Medium acidity, soft tannin, taste evolution, and pleasantly sweet and sweaty finish.
