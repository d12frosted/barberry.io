** Ratings

*** 2022-11-10 - ☆ 8.75

A 'vertical' tasting of Bruno Paillard Première Cuvée NV. This bottle was disgorged in February 2012. 36 months on lees. Complex and beautiful bouquet: chalk, croissants, candied zest and Jerez. Round, almost perfectly balanced, with a long evolving and flavourful aftertaste. Great mousse. Friendly and generous palate.
