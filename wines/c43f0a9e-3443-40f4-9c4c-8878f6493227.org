** Ratings

*** 2023-05-19 - ☆ 8.60

Bottle #7 on [[barberry:/posts/2023-05-19-bourgogne][Bourgogne Party]] organised by Vova Ulianov. The list of wines was open, but all of them were tasted blindly.

This bottle confused me a lot. It reminded me of Puligny or Chablis yet with a twist. Since it didn't remind the style of De Moor, I guessed the producer yet not the wine. Complex and intriguing wine. Fizzy and a bit funky. Bruised apples, cider, lemon and yeas (hey, autolysis). Vibrant, mineral, balanced and persistent. A beautiful bottle.
