** Ratings

*** 2022-09-02 - ☆ 8.50

Incredible vintage. In only two years and it became so beautiful, elegant, and precise. Sophisticated and perfumed bouquet: violets, raspberry, pomegranate, and earthy notes. It's a pure joy to drink this wine, so delicious and balanced it is. The sad part is the crazy price.

Wine #6 on volcanic wines tasting by Oleksandr Meier.
