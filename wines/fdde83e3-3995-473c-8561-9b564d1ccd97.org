** Ratings

*** 2023-10-13 - ☆ 7.40

The Valdebarón from Bodegas Olarra Ondarre offers an accessible gateway into the world of white Riojas, both in terms of price and availability. The nose reveals an inviting blend of honey, apple, butter, and tropical nuances. However, peel away its lees and oak influences and the wine feels somewhat exposed. Although it veers towards a slightly muted freshness, evoking the contrast between a freshly cut apple and one left out for a while, it compensates with admirable volume and concentration. For its price, it stands out as a commendable option for casual, everyday enjoyment.
