** Ratings

*** 2020-11-10 - ☆ 8.50

Amazing rosé! Just think about it - 10 years old rosé! Complex aroma, butter, rose petals, grapefruit, almonds and caramel. Refreshing with long aftertaste. Simply wow.

*** 2021-05-06 - ☆ 8.50

Well, having a chance to taste this cosmic rosé again is something. Wonderful bouquet, caramel, red flowers, grapefruit, butter. Complex, tasty, balanced with long aftertaste. Not your typical rosé, but rather a bomb!
