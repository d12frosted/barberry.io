*** 2022-08-15 - ☆ 7.40

Hehe, La Solana is like kissing an erupting volcano. Wait, stop imagining it! Bear with me for a while. In the nose, it has little fruits, mostly SO2, spices, wet cellar and pomegranate. Tasting it is like licking a stone covered with moss and shit (?!). Damn, SO2. Other than that, it's delicate and tasty. Maybe it just needs much more aeration. Not sure what's wrong with me, but I would like to taste it again.
