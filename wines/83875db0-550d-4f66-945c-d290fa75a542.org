** Ratings

*** 2023-08-07 - ☆ 7.50

In a blind tasting, I guessed the grape and vintage, but it took me a while to figure out it was from Jura. My first thought was Burgundy. A bit heavy and viscous, it offers a bouquet full of camomile, honey, baked pears and canned pineapple. Not complex yet delicious, it feels like hot and sick air a moment before the storm.
