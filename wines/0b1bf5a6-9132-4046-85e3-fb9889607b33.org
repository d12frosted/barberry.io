** Ratings

*** 2022-09-02 - ☆ 7.80

Soave, huh? This bottle took my expectations, shredded them and set them on fire. All thanks to its rich and complex profile. It literally yells with honey, toffee, and white berries. It has a wonderful volume. The taste is salty and slightly sweet (like cheese, you know?). Unfortunately, the aftertaste is rather short. Still, it's an incredible Soave for a decent price.

Wine #1 on volcanic wines tasting by Oleksandr Meier.
