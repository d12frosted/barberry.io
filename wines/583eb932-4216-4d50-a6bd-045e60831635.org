** Producer

Alessandro Viola is a small winery owning 7 hectares of vineyard planted with grapes Grillo, Nero d'Avola, Nerello Mascalese and Catarratto. It is located in Trapani province, near Alcamo city in Pietra district and just on the rest of Mount Bonifitato.

** Ratings

*** 2023-08-12 - ☆ 8.25

Oh my, what a delicious treatment. A complex bouquet of apricot jam (you can feel the strings of the falling fruit), persimmon, canned peach, caramel and a bit of VA. Acidity nicely balances the sweetness level. Multilayered and persistent. Nice.
