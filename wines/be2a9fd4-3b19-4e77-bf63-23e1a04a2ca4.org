** Ratings

*** 2022-09-02 - ☆ 7.25

Tasting new releases from Hummel. Csury is the craziest of the line. Still fizzy, and IMO in a bad way. It bursts with sour cherry that decided to begin its decaying process (oh those independent cherries!). VA, barnyard and yeast. But it's delightfully fresh, flavourful and overall juicy. If only it wasn't so fizzy! Maybe good for parties.
