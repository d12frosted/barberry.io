** Ratings

*** 2022-08-19 - ☆ 5.50

Tasted blind. My guess was some crippled and cheap Chardonnay from Jura. It's like it spent some time under flor, but unintentionally. Jerez-like bouquet with prevalent nuts, wet wood, vanilla, yellow plum, and green cherry plum. Lacks balance and focus. They say that maybe something happened to this specific bottle cause this wine wasn't like this before. Well, I will not hunt this wine on purpose.
