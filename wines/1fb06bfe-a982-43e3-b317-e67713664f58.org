** Producer

Jokingly they say that I am brand ambassador of Vino di Anna. It all boils down to my love for their wines. And how I force people to drink them.

** Ratings

*** 2023-07-04 - ☆ 6.50

Quite a long time passed since the last time I tasted a Vino di Anna. Here comes the new release. A crazy blend of Grecanico, Riesling, Chenin Blanc, Carricante, Catarratto and Minnella - all from Etna. Result? Restrained and simple. Apples with raw cashew. Fresh apple juice. Maybe over time, it will improve, but today it's not convincing.
