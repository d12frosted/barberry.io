** Ratings

*** 2023-01-31 - ☆ 7.50

Wine #4 on [[barberry:/posts/2023-01-31-norm-wines][Norm Wines Vol. 1]].

A little bit too shy (especially after [[barberry:/wines/e8f282e6-b655-435b-91e3-1966dbde5b25][Nozze d'Oro]]) but opens up beautifully. It's hard to guess maceration here, but no wonder - the skin contact was short, and (I guess) the age kicks in. Amber (from the cherry tree), mildew, mushrooms and a little bit of lime. The acidity is good. The palate is fresh and juicy, and the aftertaste is long and delicious.
