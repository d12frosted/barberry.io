** Ratings

*** 2022-08-08 - ☆ 7.50

Hehe, people warned me that this wine is not good. I've tasted two bottles: one was slightly fizzy, the other was good. My review is based on the better bottle. Reductive notes (we used the 'bearded man who shat himself' phrase), ascorbic candy, cherry, and raw meat. On the palate, it's elegant, fresh and overall easy-going and pleasant.
