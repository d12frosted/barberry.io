** Ratings

*** 2022-09-02 - ☆ 7.40

Tasting new releases from Hummel. I don't understand what grape is used here, probably Portugieser. Nevertheless, a nice fruit-forward bouquet full of forest berries, smokey notes, and VA. Again, the latter plays well by adding complexity and volume. Warm and long aftertaste. Slightly lacks in the balances, good otherwise.
