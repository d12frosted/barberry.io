** Ratings

*** 2022-09-13 - ☆ 8.00

A lovely blend of Pinot Noir and Gamay. Dried red flowers, hibiscus, cherry, barberry, bark, and musk. Slightly fizzy yet not disturbing. Good balance, juicy palate, fresh and lively. Velvety tannin, long aftertaste.
