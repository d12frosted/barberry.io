** Ratings

*** 2020-04-02 - ☆ 4.00

Opens up with pleasant aroma of bubblegum and strawberry. After some time there are hints of acetone. But the taste... it's really imbalanced. Acidity is too high (and I love high acidity). It's like eating green technical grapes. With similar flavours and aftertaste.

*** 2020-04-05 - ☆ 2.00

It's hard for me to find words that would describe this atrocity. Unbalanced crazy beverage.

*** 2020-04-08 - ☆ 5.00

That would be a lesson for me. Never buy shady bottles in multiple quantities before tasting them. Every bottle is different though. Could not drink, but at least used as marinade.

*** 2020-04-15 - ☆ 1.00

At last, it's the only bottle left and can finally close this chapter.
