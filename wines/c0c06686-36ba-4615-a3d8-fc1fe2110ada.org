Nerello Mascalese comes from the northern slopes of Etna - Contrada Sciaranuova, Piano Dario, Rampante. Vines were planted in 2004 and 2007. Wine is fermented for 6 days and aged in 25 hl oak casks for 12 months.

** Producer

Wine producer from Sicilia, Italy. They cultivate grapes in the vineyards of 5 different estates:

- Tenuta Regaleali
- Tenuta Capofaro
- Tenuta Tascante
- Fondazione Whitaker
- Tenuta Sallier de La Tour
