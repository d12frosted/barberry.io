** Producer

Julien Mareschal owns 5 hectares in Arbois. Around 3.5 hectares are planted with Chardonnay, Savagnin Savagnin. Trousseau, Pinot Noir and Ploussard occupy the remaining 1.5 ha. All vines are more than 30 years old.

** Ratings

*** 2022-12-09 - ☆ 8.00

Savagnin ouillé that spent 24 months on lees. No wonder bouquet is full of fruits, honey, citrus, and fresh nuts. It offers a fresh medium body, a good round structure, depth and a long flavourful and saline aftertaste. Not simple.
