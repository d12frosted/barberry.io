** Ratings

*** 2023-04-20 - ☆ 7.00

Simple and a bit vulgar in the nose. White peaches, white apples and fruit trees blossoms. Lacks balance. At least it has a pleasant citrus touch in the aftertaste. Overall, it's just a table wine I would enjoy drinking on the seashore. It's not bad per se.
