** Ratings

*** 2023-05-16 - ☆ 8.00

It was a bit tainted, but good nevertheless. A beautiful bouquet of dried cherries, pomegranate, leather, barnyard and balsamic. Powerful, even harsh mouthfeel. It's like licking sandpaper covered in cherry syrup and balsamic. Long and warm aftertaste. Good balance. Would love to taste this wine in 5+ years.
