Mind blowing wine by gorgeous Stella di Campalto. Elegant and precise, field blend of Sangiovese, Tannat, Arinarnoa, Marcelan and Lagrein of various vintages. Wild strawberry, raspberry, red flowers with truffles and salami on the back. Perfectly balanced and silky. Easy to spend hours with a glass of sexy and juicy wine like this.
