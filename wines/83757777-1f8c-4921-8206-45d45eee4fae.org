*** 2023-03-31 - ☆ 8.75

Just mindblowing. Powerful and sophisticated. A charming bouquet of quince, wet stones, gunpowder, and tilia honey. Nuanced, detailed and perfectly balanced. The aftertaste is long and persistent. A wine to drink and to enjoy without any buts (single 't').

*** 2023-04-14 - ☆ 8.75

Pfff, typical Jacky Blot. Sophisticated, nuanced and perfectly balanced. A mixture of quince, wet stones, tilia honey, gunpowder and white flowers. A long and delicious aftertaste with flavours of lemon, quince, wet pebbles and honey. Rejoice!
