** Ratings

*** 2022-01-12 - ☆ 8.50

Tasted blind and though about Chardonnay from Jura. After some thinking guessed producer. Beautiful Godello that went through oxidation. Clear pale straw colour. Powerful and complex bouquet showing some age: citrus, salted nuts, baked apples and wet stones. Crisply fresh, well balanced with pronounced flavours and long aftertaste. Delicious and mineral.
