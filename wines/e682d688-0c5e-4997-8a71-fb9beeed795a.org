** Ratings

*** 2020-09-18 - ☆ 7.00

Massive brett attack backed up by red fruits and tomato juice. It tried to sneak in some VA hints, and there is no VA police, so it succeeded. After some time, reductive base is still there, but it becomes more fruity, with cranberry and tomato juice, and some freshly made leather on the back. Pretty plain taste, not overwhelming.
