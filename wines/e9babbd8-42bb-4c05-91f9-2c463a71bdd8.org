** Ratings

*** 2023-10-09 - ☆ 8.50

The nose is an effervescent celebration of citrus, offering a lively mix of grapefruit, lemon, and nuanced hints of orange oil. These bright notes are complemented by undertones of green apple, almond, and a rich lemon curd. Moving to the palate, the wine showcases a medium body. Its pronounced acidity is both robust and pointed, which, to my delight, harmonises beautifully with the fruit-driven finish. Overall, this wine is a testament to its character, leaving a lasting impression of its intrinsic quality.
