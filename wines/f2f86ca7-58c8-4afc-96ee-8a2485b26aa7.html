Producer

Julien Mareschal owns 5 hectares in Arbois. Around 3.5 hectares are planted with Chardonnay, Savagnin Savagnin. Trousseau, Pinot Noir and Ploussard occupy the remaining 1.5 ha. All vines are more than 30 years old.

Ratings

2022-12-09 -  8.25

Oh, the famous Vin Jaune. A love or hate style of wine. A pure pleasure to witness the reaction of people tasting it for the first time. This particular bottle is not very complex but offers a typical fino aroma mixed with citrus, bread, and curry. A fresh and mineral palate. A profound and long finish.
