Famous producer from Argentina. They say that Catena family has resurrected Malbec.

** Ratings

*** 2021-08-13 - ☆ 7.50

The most austere wine of the range. Pine nuts, parsley, blackberry and black cherry. Well balanced, good structure and so damn juicy making it quaffable. Great QPR.
