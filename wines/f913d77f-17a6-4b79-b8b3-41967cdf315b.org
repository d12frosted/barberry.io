** Producer

Alfredo Arribas, the same person behind [[barberry:/producers/824f09b6-4bf5-43fa-8d2f-09eb5092be8a][Portal del Priorat]], created his second wine project in Montsant DO and called it Trossos.

** Ratings

*** 2022-10-10 - ☆ 8.00

Lovely and friendly Cariñena. It doesn't bite you when you try to pet it. Lots of black fruits, chocolate, earth and spices. Medium+ body, but round, balanced and overall sophisticated. But most importantly, delicious.

*** 2023-03-14 - ☆ 8.00

Funky and interesting Cariñena. It bursts with barnyard notes at the beginning. But oxygen is a miracle worker. A multi-layered and sophisticated bouquet of black fruits, chocolate, earth and spices. And while some of the aromas are not for everyone, the palate is well-balanced, round and mature. Medium-full body. Delicious.
