** Ratings

*** 2023-03-17 - ☆ 8.25

Tasting this wine after a set of Première Cuvée by Bruno Paillard. And I must admit, it was a good idea. Lemon curd, Jerez, baked apple, cream and smoke. Fresh, round and delicious (IMO atypical) Chablis with a long and flavourful aftertaste.
