*** 2023-08-11 - ☆ 8.00

Wine #4 on [[barberry:/posts/2023-08-11-mythopia][a blind tasting dedicated to Mythopia]].

What a funny wine. The first sniff reveals canned sardines. With time the gang becomes bigger - hibiscus, VA and smoked oak. Nice structure, velvety, racy and delicious. Medium body and good fruit. This wine is more interesting to drink than to sniff.
