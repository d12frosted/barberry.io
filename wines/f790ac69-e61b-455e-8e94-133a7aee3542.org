** Ratings

*** 2023-03-22 - ☆ 7.25

Euphoria was the most impressive wine of the evening. Not sure if it's a good thing to have in your glass, but Euphoria smells like calendula. And I love this aroma. It also features bruised apples and caramel. A pleasant mouthfeel with good acidity and tannin. But it lacks confidence. Definitely going to buy some other bottles from this small winery.
