** Producer

Member of "bande des cinq", one of the beginners of natural wine movement in Beaujolais.

** Ratings

*** 2022-11-18 - ☆ 8.00

I wanted to taste it blind, but someone spoiled the appellation. Yet, I guessed the producer. An interesting bouquet with red berries, spoiled fruits, a wet forest floor and VA. The latter also manifests as an elevator, making this Gamay fresh, vibrant and more complex. Great style, relatively funky.

*** 2023-07-21 - ☆ 8.25

Tasted blind while knowing the list of wines. Guessed only the appellation. And it's funny cause I tasted this wine almost a year ago and my notes are pretty much the same. A sophisticated bouquet of Nordic berries, (volatile) horse sweet, cranberry, chocolate and caramel. Juicy, fresh, vibrant, medium-bodied and stylish. Very good balance.
