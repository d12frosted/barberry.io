100% Pinot Noir produced conventionally. The wine was aged in Seguin Moreau barrels for 6 months. 9800 bottles produced.

** Producer

Novak Winery is a winery founded in 2004 on the basis of the old enterprise from 1977 in the village of Tartaul, Cantemir district of the Republic of Moldova. The vineyards from which the wines are produced stretch over 30 hectares in IGP Valul lui Traian. The vineyards are planted with local and international varieties, and the winery pays more attention to older local varieties.

** Ratings

*** 2023-07-18 - ☆ 7.50

Wine #4 on [[barberry:/posts/2023-07-18-moldova][Vinul Moldovei Vol. 1]].

Generous and ripe new-world-styled Pinot Noir. Sweet cherry, dark chocolate, earth, generic tobacco (herbaceous notes) and well-done steak. Aye, a bit smokey. And while it may charm you with its aromas, the palate may kill you and your descendants. Rustic dry tannins and high alcohol. You may love it, you may hate it, but probably you'll enjoy this wine with some heavy food. A lovely and interesting bouquet with a questionable palate.
