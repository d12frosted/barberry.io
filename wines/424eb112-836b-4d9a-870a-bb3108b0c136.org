** Ratings

*** 2023-06-06 - ☆ 8.60

Wine #5 on [[barberry:/posts/2023-06-06-rose][Rose Petals Party Vol. 2]].

This is an incredible 16-years-old rosé. Sophisticated and multilayered. Dehydrated strawberry, caramel, crème brûlée, nuts, peach-flavoured ice tea, toast and bitter herbs. Good acidity, flavourful, almost perfectly balanced, and persistent. And a great value.
