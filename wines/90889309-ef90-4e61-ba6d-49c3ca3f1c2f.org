** Ratings

*** 2023-06-16 - ☆ 8.00

Wine #2 on [[barberry:/posts/2023-06-16-jura][Jura Party by Vova Ulianov]].

What a lovely blend of Pinot Noir and Ploussard. An aromatic and youthful bouquet of Nordic red berries, blood, and wild strawberries on a VA canvas. It's light, fresh, well-balanced and sophisticated. The aftertaste is long with flavours of raw meat, Nordic red berries, and bakery spices with a metallic and salty finish.
