** Ratings

*** 2022-10-14 - ☆ 8.25

Wow, a nifty Carignan with some great value. Complex bouquet: leather, forest berries, blood, brett, jam, and red flowers. Almost ideally balanced, juicy, voluminous and multilayered. The tannin is still young but so well integrated. A long aftertaste with flavours of violets and (dark) cherry.
