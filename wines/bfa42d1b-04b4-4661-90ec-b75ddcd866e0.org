** Ratings

*** 2023-02-18 - ☆ 8.25

Stylish wine. Thanks to 2 years in the bottle after disgorgement, it offers freshly baked croissants with butter in addition to restrained minerality. Voluminous, vinous and long aftertaste. The balance is close to ideal. It has nice bitternes in the finish with flavours of Jerez and iodine.
