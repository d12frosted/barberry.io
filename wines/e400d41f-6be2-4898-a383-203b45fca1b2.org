** Producer

Julien Mareschal owns 5 hectares in Arbois. Around 3.5 hectares are planted with Chardonnay, Savagnin Savagnin. Trousseau, Pinot Noir and Ploussard occupy the remaining 1.5 ha. All vines are more than 30 years old.

** Ratings

*** 2023-03-31 - ☆ 7.75

It all starts with blood sausages and barnyard. With time the brett-y notes calm down to reveal spoiled raspberry, cranberry, barberry candy, and red flowers. The blood is still there. Delicate, juicy and calm. Not fascinating per se, just a pleasant wine.
