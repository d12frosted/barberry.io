** Ratings

*** 2020-09-18 - ☆ 7.00

Pretty nice Pet Nat featuring cranberry and red currant with brett touch. Leveraged acidity, refreshing. Lovely for picnics.

*** 2020-10-23 - ☆ 6.25

Life doesn't end when you can't find Pet Nat I was advised to taste. So here comes this light Rose Pet Nat, moderately fruity with pleasant rotten hints. Easy to open, easy to drink, easy to forget and switch to something better. Cheers!
