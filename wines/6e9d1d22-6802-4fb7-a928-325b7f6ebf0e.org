** Ratings

*** 2021-08-13 - ☆ 7.50

Grüner Veltliner is a rare grape on my table. According to my notes, the last time I tasted a wine made exclusively of this grape was June of 2019. So I decided to change that by taking a bottle from wonderful producer. This one is aromatic and almost perfumed - pear, green unripe apricot, cherry blossoms. Crunchy, light and easy going.
