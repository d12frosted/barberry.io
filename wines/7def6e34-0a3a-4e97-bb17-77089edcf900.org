** Ratings

*** 2022-12-02 - ☆ 7.00

I tasted it blind but didn't have a chance to guess as it was not right. It has a restrained bouquet with notes of chocolate crumbles, VA and porter beer (or Borodino bread). The issue with this wine manifests primarily in the taste: unpleasant bitterness and overly short aftertaste. Yet, it's flavourful: bitter medicinal herbs and porter beer.
