** Ratings

*** 2023-10-03 - ☆ 7.50

Momento Mori keeps surprising with their grape choice. A blend of Schioppettino, Syrah and (apparently) Malvasia. The result is not very complex nor fascinating, but it's a nice glue glue. Spoiled berries, horse sweat and chocolate. It's a white whine in a red dress.
