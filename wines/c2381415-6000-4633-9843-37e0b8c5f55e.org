** Ratings

*** 2021-05-21 - ☆ 7.25

Crazy and vulgar orange Pinot Blanc. Chamomile with pumpkin, oil, apple, tea and nectarine. My descriptions are far more fascinating than the wine itself, which is straightforward, yet flavourful. Aftertaste is like I've consumed a pile of rotten orange peel. Which is not bad per se.
