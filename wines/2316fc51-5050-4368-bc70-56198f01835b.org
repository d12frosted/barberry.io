** Ratings

*** 2023-05-26 - ☆ 7.40

A pleasant tropical bouquet with mango (hehe, carrot), passion fruit, elderflower, fennel and traces of spoilage. Easy to drink, friendly and not complex. IMO, the acidity is lacking, as well as the aftertaste length. But it's a good summertime drink.
