** Producer

Egly-Ouriet produces Champagne primarily from Grand Cru village of Ambonnay located in Montagne de Reims. Ambonnay is known as the top village for Pinot Noir. Egly uses Organic Farming, his Pinot Noir and Chardonnay grapes are 50 years old on average. Wines are aged extensively on the lees (up to 10 years).

** Ratings

*** 2023-04-06 - ☆ 8.50

A combination of delicacy and character. A charming bouquet of red flowers, cherry, red berries, dry earth, and cloves. Austere mouthfeel with high acidity, tight tannin (reminiscent of the walnut husk), a long and flavourful aftertaste and a mineral-fruity-flowery finish. Sophisticated and delicious.
