** Ratings

*** 2023-07-04 - ☆ 7.75

Wine #1 on [[barberry:/posts/2023-07-04-mixed-bag][Mixed Bag Vol. 8]].

It just arrived, so I reckon it should improve over the next few weeks. But even today, it's charming in its oxidative bouquet of baked lemon, mushroom, baked pears, dried apples, bread crusts and nuts. Fresh, intense at the start, evolving and complex. It offers attractive flavours of lemon and baked apples topped with honey. Lacks a bit in the aftertaste hence the lower score. Will taste it again later.
